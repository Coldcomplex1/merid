AI context check (beta)

Optional. After Merid replaces words on a page, Google Gemini (flash-lite) double-checks that each replaced word fits its sentence - doubtful words are reverted back to the original text automatically. Uses your own free API key from aistudio.google.com (create one there, then paste it below).

Data & privacy

  • Page text is scanned locally in your browser to find vocabulary matches.
  • By default the extension makes no network requests - it never sends page content anywhere and uses no AI API.
  • If you enable the optional AI context check, only short sentence snippets around replaced words are sent to Google Gemini using your own API key. Your key is stored on this device; if you sign in, it is also backed up privately to your Merid account (readable only by you) so it follows you across devices.
  • Only your settings (selected dataset, display mode, intensity, on/off) are stored on your device via chrome.storage.
  • Turn the extension off any time from the popup.
