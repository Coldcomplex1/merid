Get the most out of Merid ✨

Turn on AI Context Check and Merid checks that every swapped word fits its sentence. Words that do not fit change back on their own. All you need is your own free Gemini API key, pasted once:

  1. Open aistudio.google.com/apikey and sign in with Google.
  2. Click Create API key, then copy the key (it starts with "AIza…").
  3. In Merid Settings → AI context check, paste the key and press Save key.
